Angry Dragon

Simulation 2020

How To Play

Explore the island and take back your treasure by destroying enchanted villages. Consume meteors with dragon fire to regain dragon health. Gain enough treasure to level up your dragon. Click TAB to visit the village store and upgrad your dragon.

Game controls

Welcome to our enchanted fantasy world

Fly around the map using the fire breathing dragon to burn down villages and reclaim your rightful fortune.

Consume meteors with dragon fire to regain dragon health.

Moving The Dragon

WASD: forward, back, left, and right

L-shift: Land Dragon

Space: Ascend Dragon

Mouse0/L-Click: FlameThrower Attack

Tab: Upgrade Screen Or Exit Game
