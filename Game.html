Angry Dragon

Simulation 2020

How To Play

Explore the island and take back your treasure to defeat the castle by destroying enchanted villages. Burn meteors to increase your dragon health. Gain enough treasure to level up your dragon.

Game controls

wasd: forward, back, turn left and turn right

L-shift: Land Dragon

Space: Ascend Dragon

Mouse0/L-Click: FlameThrower Attack

Tab: Upgrade Screen Or Exit Game
